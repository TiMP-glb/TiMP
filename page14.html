Knowledge is Growth

July 12, 2020

As we continue these much needed teachings on the tough topic of racial issues and systemic injustices. We all must do our part to take action towards overcoming the cowardly voices of ignorance and hate. My hope is that around the world those voices of right and more importantly righteousness will ultimately prevail. If you have a platform, especially those of us in Christ and you are being silent on this issue. Then you are doing yourself and those you serve a disservice.

Even if you don’t understand how to approach the discussion then reach out to find someone who does. The world will never be perfect because we are all flawed imperfect people. But it’s also a generality that not having knowledge is very detrimental
